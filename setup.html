Steps To Follow

1

Watch the setup video

Watch the complete walkthrough once before starting. It shows the AWS setup and how to open your personal Stockkar app.

2

Create or open your AWS account

Use your own AWS account. This keeps your app, broker connection, and static IP under your control.

3

Upload the setup file

Upload the YAML file in AWS CloudFormation and follow the steps shown in the video.

4

Enter app name, PIN, and email

Use a simple app name, set a private update PIN, and enter your email for alerts. Leave other settings unchanged.

5

Open your Stockkar app link

When setup completes, open Outputs and click AppUrl. That is your personal Stockkar link.

6

Connect broker and start

Paste the static IP in your broker account, save tokens inside Stockkar, fetch screener, configure algo, and start monitoring.

Tip: after AWS setup, check your email and confirm the AWS alert subscription.
